CELL ORGANELLES:-

NUCLEUS:
It is the central round part of cell.It contains all the genetic material of cell.It is also called the Brain of Cell.
ENDOPLASMIC RETICULUM
Its function is to produce proteins for the rest of the cell to function. It is of two tppes:-Rough Endoplamic Reticulum(have rybosomes)& Smooth Endoplamic Reticulum(do not have rybosomes)
MYTOCHONDRIA:
It is the power bank of cell.It plays a very important role in sexual reproduction.
CELL MEMBRANE:
It helps in seperating one cell from the other.
CELL WALL:
It is like a protection wall after the cell membrane.It is present in plant cells because plants can't move.
CYTOPLASM:
It is a jelly-like substance present between nucleus and cell membrane.Or we can say that it is a semi-fluid on which all organelles are present except cell membrane and cell wall.
VACUOLE:
Vacuoles are the empty spaces present in the cell.These are small and sometimes even not present in animal cell but plant cell has a very large vacuole.

2.CROMATIN FIBRES

3.NUCLEOPLASM
semi-fluid present between nucleolus and nuclaer membrane
4.CHROMOSOMES
Chromosomes are sets of packed DNA.Each chromosome has 2 meter of packed DNA.
5.DNA
DNA stands for deoxyribonucleic acid. it is the hereditary material in humans and almost all other organisms.
6.GENE(s)
Gene helps to transfer characterstics of parents to offspring(like-brown hairs,blue eyes,etc).
